Our Aims & Objectives

  • Training and capacity building, in evidence based prevention strategies.
  • Promote the campaign against substance and drug abuse in communities and schools.
  • Collaborate with school authorities, youth organizations, religious bodies, and relevant government agencies.
  • Educate, rehabilitate, and re-integrate affected individuals after correctional processes.
  • Empower youth and women through initiatives to discourage substance abuse.
  • Encourage sports and skill development activities to guide youth positively.
